About textract.me

textract.me is an online AI-powered OCR (Optical Character Recognition) and translation platform. It allows you to extract text from any image and instantly translate it into multiple languages — all within seconds.

Whether you're digitizing documents, translating menus, or saving notes from screenshots — textract.me makes the process fast, accurate, and effortless.

Our Mission

Our goal is to make AI text recognition and translation accessible to everyone — students, travelers, researchers, and professionals. No ads, no distractions — just simple and reliable tools.

Powered by AI

textract.me uses advanced machine learning models to recognize text from images and translate it accurately. We combine OCR and neural translation technologies for the best possible results.

Why Choose textract.me?

  • ✔️ Fast and secure image processing
  • ✔️ Supports 100+ languages for OCR and translation
  • ✔️ Clean, ad-free interface
  • ✔️ Optional PDF export and text-to-speech tools
